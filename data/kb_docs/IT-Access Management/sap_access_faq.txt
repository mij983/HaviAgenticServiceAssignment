TEAM: IT-Access Management
SAP PASSWORD RESET AND ACCESS FAQ
==================================

This document answers the most common SAP access and password questions
raised by end users to the IT Service Desk.

---

Q: I cannot log in to SAP. It says my password has expired.

A: SAP passwords expire every 90 days by company policy.
   To reset your SAP password:
   1. Go to the SAP self-service portal: https://sap-portal.company.com
   2. Click "Forgot Password" and verify your identity with your employee ID
   3. A temporary password will be sent to your registered email
   4. Log in with the temporary password and set a new one immediately
   If you cannot access the portal, contact IT-Access Management and provide
   your SAP user ID and employee number.

---

Q: I need access to a new SAP module (e.g. MM, FI, SD, WM).

A: SAP module access must be approved by your line manager.
   Steps:
   1. Your manager raises an access request in the IT portal
   2. The request is reviewed by IT-Access Management
   3. Once approved, the role is assigned within 2 business days
   Do NOT share your SAP credentials with colleagues. Each user must have
   their own named account.

---

Q: I locked my SAP account after too many failed login attempts.

A: Accounts are locked after 5 consecutive failed login attempts.
   Contact IT-Access Management to unlock your account.
   Provide: your SAP user ID, your full name, and your manager's name.
   Unlocks are processed within 4 business hours.

---

Q: SAP is loading very slowly or timing out.

A: Slow SAP performance is handled by the SAP Basis team, not Access Management.
   Please raise this with IT-SC-EPAM-SAP Basis Support.
   Include: your SAP client number, transaction code you are running,
   and the time the issue occurred.

---

Q: I need a SAP user created for a new employee.

A: New user creation requests must come from HR via the onboarding workflow.
   If the account is urgent, contact IT-Access Management directly with the
   employee's full name, start date, job role, and required SAP modules.

---

ROUTING GUIDE FOR SERVICE DESK

  SAP login / password issues     -> IT-Access Management
  SAP performance / slow loading  -> IT-SC-EPAM-SAP Basis Support
  SAP workflow approval stuck     -> IT-SC-EPAM-SAP Workflow
  SAP functional issues (FI/MM)   -> IT-SC-EPAM-SAP-AMS-Support
  SAP technical errors / dumps    -> IT-SC-EPAM-SAP Basis Support
  New SAP user creation           -> IT-Access Management
